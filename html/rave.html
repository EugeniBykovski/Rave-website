SAFETY RAFTS

Mackay's professional guides will guide you along your journey in a variety of rafts and toys. On each trip we offer oar boats, paddleboats, inflatable kayaks and a variety of water toys. All Mackay guides have Swift Water Rescue Certifications. Mackay has an EMT on every trip. We also carry satellite phones, defibrillators and full trauma kits. Safety is number one.
